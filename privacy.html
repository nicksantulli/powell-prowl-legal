Privacy Policy

Last updated: —

The short version

Powell Prowl: Rate Chase does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no analytics, no trackers, no third-party SDKs, and no advertising networks.

What stays on your device

  • Your unlocked level number and best score (saved in iOS UserDefaults so you can pick up where you left off).
  • Your audio, haptics, and difficulty preferences.
  • A lifetime "whack" counter used by the Game Center leaderboard.

All of the above is stored locally on your device. We never see it. Uninstalling the app or tapping Reset all progress in Settings clears it.

Game Center

If you are signed into Apple's Game Center, the app submits leaderboard scores (total score, fastest tag, lifetime whacks) to Apple. Game Center data is handled directly by Apple under Apple's privacy policy — we do not receive or store any of it.

House ads

Between levels the app occasionally shows in-house promotional cards for HomeLight products (HELOC, Buy Before You Sell, lender portal). These cards are built into the app — they are not served by an ad network and do not load any tracking code. Tapping a card opens the relevant HomeLight web page in your default browser; what happens after that is governed by HomeLight's privacy policy.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. There are no accounts, profiles, or in-app messaging.

Changes

If this policy ever changes materially, the new version will be posted at this URL with an updated date above. Continued use of the app after a change constitutes acceptance.
